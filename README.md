# Campus Clique

## Overview

Campus Clique is a mobile application concept designed to help college students build meaningful connections, discover campus events, join communities, and stay engaged with campus life.

The project focuses on solving the common challenges students face in:

- Making new friends
- Finding relevant events
- Discovering student clubs
- Building campus communities
- Improving student engagement

---

## Problem Statement

Students often struggle to connect with peers outside their immediate circles. Important campus events, clubs, and networking opportunities frequently go unnoticed due to fragmented communication channels.

How might we create a centralized platform that helps students connect, engage, and participate in campus activities effortlessly?

---

## Solution

Campus Clique provides:

- Student Profiles
- Event Discovery
- Club Communities
- Campus Feed
- Messaging & Networking
- Personalized Recommendations

## User Persona

First-year engineering student.

Goals:
- Meet new people
- Join clubs
- Attend events

Pain Points:
- Doesn't know where events are posted
- Finds it difficult to connect with seniors
- Misses important campus opportunities
